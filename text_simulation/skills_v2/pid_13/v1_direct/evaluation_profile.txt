## Intertemporal patience

For example, they prefer $6 in 6 weeks over $5.50 in 5 weeks but prefer $5.50 in 5 weeks over $6 in 6 weeks when the difference is larger. This pattern indicates moderate patience with some present bias but willingness to wait for larger rewards. The respondent’s choices reflect a balanced intertemporal preference rather than extreme impatience or extreme patience.
