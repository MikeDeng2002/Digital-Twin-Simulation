Overall, the participant exhibits a moderate maximizer profile with a tendency to re-evaluate options but settle on good-enough choices rather than exhaustive search or perfectionism.

- Denominator neglect: Low susceptibility, as participant correctly interprets probabilities and percentages in financial literacy tasks.  
- Belief bias: Low to moderate; participant shows good logical reasoning but some errors in spatial tasks suggest occasional heuristic shortcuts.  
- Wason matching bias: Low, as participant correctly identifies cards to test conditional rules.

**LOW CONFIDENCE**
