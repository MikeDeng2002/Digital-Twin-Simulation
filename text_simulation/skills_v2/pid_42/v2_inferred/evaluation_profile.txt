She is likely to plan and save rather than seek immediate consumption.

## Risk preferences

For losses, she also shows risk aversion, preferring to accept certain smaller losses rather than lotteries with higher expected losses. She rejects unfair offers in ultimatum games and prefers to keep a moderate share in dictator games, indicating moderate risk aversion consistent with her moderate income and cautious financial stance. She also correctly understands financial literacy questions, indicating good comprehension of risk and return.
