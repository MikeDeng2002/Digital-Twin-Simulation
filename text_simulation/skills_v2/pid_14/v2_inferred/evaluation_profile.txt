This indicates relatively high intertemporal patience. This is consistent with her moderate to high Conscientiousness (agreeing strongly with being organized, efficient, practical, and accurate) and her self-reported planning and perseverance traits. She shows a capacity for delayed gratification and planning, which supports prudent financial decision-making.

She accepts some risk but values certainty as amounts increase. For losses, she prefers to avoid large losses and accepts smaller certain losses over risky larger losses, consistent with typical loss aversion. Her financial literacy is moderate, understanding basic investment and risk concepts. Income is relatively high, but unemployment and disability may temper risk-taking. Overall, she exhibits moderate risk aversion with some tolerance for risk in gains but cautious about losses.

## Social preferences
