## Risk preferences  
The survey includes multiple lottery and risk preference tasks. The respondent consistently prefers the lottery over smaller certain amounts until the certain amount approaches the expected value, indicating moderate risk aversion. For example, in a 50% chance to win $6 lottery, she prefers the lottery over amounts up to about $2.75 but switches to the certain amount at $3.00 and above.
