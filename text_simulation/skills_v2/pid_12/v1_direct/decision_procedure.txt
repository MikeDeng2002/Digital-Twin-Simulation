## Intertemporal patience

The person consistently chooses the larger, later reward when the amounts are close (e.g., $6 in 6 weeks over $3 in 5 weeks, $8 in 7 weeks over $4 in 6 weeks, $10 in 7 weeks over $5 in 5 weeks), indicating patience and willingness to delay gratification. This pattern is consistent across multiple similar tasks, showing a patient intertemporal preference with a reasonable discounting of delayed rewards.
