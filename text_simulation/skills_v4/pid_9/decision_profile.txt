## Summary  
This participant exhibits a moderately spendthrift consumption mode, comfortable with spending but not excessively so. They tend toward maximization, searching thoroughly for the best options, and have a moderate need for cognitive closure, balancing decisiveness with some tolerance for ambiguity. Their regulatory focus is strongly promotion-oriented, making them more responsive to gain-framed choices and opportunity-driven decisions. High conscientiousness supports organized and efficient decision execution. Time preferences reveal moderate impatience and present bias, consistent with their spending and regulatory focus profiles. The most predictive single feature for simulating this person on choice tasks is their high promotion focus, which will strongly influence their responsiveness to gain versus loss framing and opportunity cues.
