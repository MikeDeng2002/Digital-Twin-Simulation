## Summary

They are moderately conscientious but show some disorganization. Metacognitively, they are strongly overconfident and overplace themselves relative to peers despite low actual cognitive ability, indicating poor calibration. Their time preferences reveal extreme impatience and moderate present bias, consistent with their spending tendencies.
