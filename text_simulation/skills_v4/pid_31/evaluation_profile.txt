The participant’s sender offer is moderately fair but not generous (typical offers are 40-50%), and their receiver behavior enforces fairness by rejecting offers below 40%, showing consistency between giving and tolerance.

### Dictator Game  
The participant sends $2 (40%) of the $5 to the other person, keeping $3. The other person has no power to reject. Participant’s thoughts: "equality," "fairness."

The Dictator offer equals the Ultimatum offer, indicating that giving is driven by fairness preferences rather than strategic considerations. The participant is willing to share a substantial portion even when not compelled by potential punishment.
