## Summary  
This participant exhibits a highly conscientious and systematic decision style, with a moderate promotion focus that orients them toward gains and achievements but balanced by a sense of duty and responsibility. They tend to satisfice rather than maximize, and have a moderate need for cognitive closure, favoring some decisiveness without extreme intolerance for ambiguity. Time preferences reveal extreme impatience and strong present bias, preferring immediate smaller rewards over larger delayed ones.
